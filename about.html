About Us

Founded in 2003, **UMG Technologies, Inc.** designs, builds, and supports high‑reliability industrial automation equipment. Our flagship pin-handling machines power production lines around the world, and our in‑house software stack leverages C#/.NET, real‑time machine control, and computer vision to deliver unmatched precision.

We partner closely with manufacturers to modernize legacy processes, improve yields, and push the boundaries of what’s possible on the factory floor.
